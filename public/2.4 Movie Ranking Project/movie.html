The Best Movies According to Neor

My Top 3 movies of all-time

Avengers End game

After the devastating events of Avengers: Infinity War (2018), the universe is in ruins. With the help of remaining allies, the Avengers assemble once more in order to reverse Thanos' actions and restore balance to the universe.

Gumraah

Sub Inspector Shivani Mathur is assigned to investigate a man's murder, and she finds two look-alike suspects, who don't know the existence of each other. Things become more complicated for Shivani when she uncovers new facts about the case.

Zindagi na Milegi Doobara

Three friends decide to turn their fantasy vacation into reality after one of their friends gets engaged. Three friends who were inseparable in childhood decide to go on a three-week-long bachelor road trip to Spain, in order to re-establish their bond and explore thrilling adventures, before one of them gets married
